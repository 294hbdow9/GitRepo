REVIEW

I watched Parasite a very long time ago with my sister and I was thinking about rewatching it again because I remembered how exciting it was the first time around. Parasite is a good film because of how it deals with issues like class inequality and the wealth gap. It was interesting to see how the director choose to tell a story about between a rich family and a less fortunate one. The film leaves you with a desire to speak to others about the issues mentioned and it does it in a captivating way.

I remember the film brought a lot of discussusion surrounding topics such as class discrimination and inequality. The actors were also very good at portraying their characters which made it really entertaining all throughout.

Who doesn't love a good commentary on the world?
